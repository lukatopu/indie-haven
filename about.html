My Mission & Vision

Elevating the voices of independent creators in a world dominated by massive budgets.

My Story

Hi, I am a solo web developer with a deep, lifelong passion for gaming. After years of playing mainstream AAA releases, I realized I was getting exhausted by the repetitive formulas, microtransactions, and lack of innovation.

Everything changed when I started exploring the indie scene. I discovered that the most creative, heart-wrenching, and mechanically perfect games were being crafted by solo developers or incredibly small, dedicated teams.

I built Indie Haven entirely on my own to help more people discover these masterpieces. My goal is to ensure that brilliant, passion-driven games like Hollow Knight and Celeste don't slip under the radar. I want to build a platform that points players directly to these unforgettable experiences.

Core Values

Art Over Profit

I prioritize games that have a distinct artistic vision rather than those designed purely for heavy monetization.

Developer First

I respect the passionate, hard work of developers. I link directly to their stores to support them 100%.

Community Driven

My recommendations are shaped by players. If the community finds a hidden gem, I want to amplify it here.
